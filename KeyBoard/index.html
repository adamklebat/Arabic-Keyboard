Arabic Keyboard Layout (PC)

This Arabic keyboard layout helps people who do not have Arabic letters on their keyboard. If your keyboard only shows English letters, this page shows exactly where Arabic letters are located.

Arabic Keyboard for English Keyboards

Where Are Arabic Letters on Keyboard?

Arabic letters are mapped to English keys on a standard QWERTY keyboard. The small English letter on each key shows the exact position on your real keyboard.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use this without installing anything?

Yes. This is a visual Arabic keyboard layout only.

Who is this for?

For anyone who does not have Arabic letters printed on their keyboard.
